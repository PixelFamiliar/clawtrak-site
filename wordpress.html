WordPress Plugin

Make your WordPress site instantly discoverable by AI agents like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. Our free plugin handles the fundamentals hands-off.

ClawTrak AI Readiness v1.0.0

Auto-generates /llms.txt, /AGENTS.md, and injects our claw.js analytics tracking so you can see which AI bots visit your website.

Download Plugin (.zip)
Note: This free plugin covers 2 of the 8 AI readiness checks. For full coverage (OpenAPI specifications, AI agent discovery cards, detailed Schema.org JSON-LD coverage), you can purchase the complete $49 AI Readiness Package.

Installation

  • Download the clawtrak-ai-readiness.zip file using the button above.
  • Log in to your WordPress Admin dashboard.
  • Go to Plugins > Add New Plugin, then click Upload Plugin at the top.
  • Select the downloaded ZIP file and click Install Now.
  • After installation, click Activate Plugin.

Configuration

  • In your WordPress Admin menu, go to Settings > ClawTrak.
  • Verify your Site ID (your domain name) is correct. This is used to track your AI analytics.
  • (Optional) In the Extra Business Info box, add any specific details, services, or pricing you want ChatGPT and Claude to know about that isn't explicitly on your pages.
  • Click Save Changes.

How it works

Once activated, the plugin dynamically creates a machine-readable profile of your business. If an AI agent attempts to read yourdomain.com/llms.txt or yourdomain.com/AGENTS.md, the plugin intercepts the request and serves standard-compliant Markdown describing your site's name, description, recent pages, posts, and AI crawler permissions.

It also injects a lightweight `<script>` tag in your footer. This analytics beacon detects known generative AI crawler bots and logs their visits to your ClawTrak dashboard.
