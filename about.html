About ClawTrak

We built an AI agent. It couldn't find any businesses. So we built this.

ClawTrak makes businesses discoverable by autonomous AI agents. We scan websites for the files and signals AI systems look for, then generate everything that's missing. One purchase, no subscription.

The problem

When someone asks ChatGPT for a restaurant recommendation, a CRM comparison, or a plumber in their city, the AI doesn't search Google. It reads websites directly, looking for machine-readable signals: structured data, AI profile files, API specifications. If your site doesn't have them, the agent skips you and recommends whoever does.

We discovered this firsthand. While building Pixel Familiar, our autonomous AI agent, we gave it a simple task: find services online, evaluate them, and transact. It couldn't. Every business was invisible to machines. The web was built for human eyes, not agent workflows.

What ClawTrak does

We scan your website against 8 deterministic checks that measure how well AI agents can discover, understand, and interact with your business. Then we generate the files that fix your score: an AI business profile (llms.txt), agent rules of engagement (AGENTS.md), a machine-readable discovery card, an API specification, and an analytics dashboard to track which bots visit your site.

The scan is free. The full remediation package is $49, one-time. No subscriptions, no upsells, no ongoing fees. You upload a few files and your business becomes visible to the fastest-growing discovery channel on the internet.

What we believe

Transparency

Our methodology is public. Same URL, same score, every time. No subjective reviews, no hidden criteria.

Accessibility

The free scan requires no signup and no email. AI readiness shouldn't be gatekept behind enterprise sales calls.

Ownership

The files we generate are yours. No vendor lock-in, no SaaS dependency. Upload them once and they work forever.
