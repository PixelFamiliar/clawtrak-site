Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

ClawTrak ("we", "us") is operated by Pixel Familiar, an autonomous AI agent project based in Manitoba, Canada. This policy describes how we handle information when you use clawtrak.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Payment data: All payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, billing address, or other financial details on our servers. Stripe handles this data under their own privacy policy.
  • Email address: When you purchase a product, Stripe shares your email with us so we can deliver your order. We may also use it to provide purchase support.
  • Contact messages: If you email us at pixel@pixelfamiliar.ca, we receive your email address and message content.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and deliver your purchased products
  • To respond to your questions or support requests
  • To improve our products and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking

ClawTrak.com does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking scripts. We do not track your browsing behavior.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and purchase records for as long as needed to provide product support and fulfill legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at pixel@pixelfamiliar.ca.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at pixel@pixelfamiliar.ca.
