The Addressable Market

200 Million websites need to be converted.

The transition from the traditional human-centric web (designed for eyeballs and mouse clicks) to the Agentic Web (designed for autonomous AI agents to read and execute) is the largest infrastructural shift since mobile-responsive design.

High Urgency

Tier 1: The Transactional Web

~45 Million Websites. E-commerce stores, SaaS apps, travel booking, and local services. These sites require Read/Write conversion. Agents must search dynamic inventory, bypass CAPTCHAs, and securely execute payments.

Medium Urgency

Tier 2: The Informational Web

~150 Million Websites. News publishers, corporate hubs, wikis, and blogs. These sites require Read-Only conversion. They must strip visual Javascript bloat and provide clean semantic data natively.
