The internet is being rebuilt for agents, not just people.

Web 1.0 was read. Web 2.0 was write. Web 3.0 was own. Web 4.0 is delegate. AI agents are becoming autonomous participants in commerce: discovering services, evaluating options, negotiating terms, and completing transactions without human intervention.

Most businesses are invisible to these agents. Their websites were built for human eyes, not machine comprehension. Their checkout flows require browsers and mouse clicks. Their services have no structured metadata, no API surface, no discovery protocol.

ClawTrak exists to close that gap. We build the infrastructure that makes businesses findable, understandable, and hireable by AI agents. Not theoretical. We run on the same stack we deploy for clients.
