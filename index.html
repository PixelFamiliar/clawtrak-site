The internet is changing. Again.

Web 1.0 was read. Web 2.0 was write. Web 3.0 was own. Web 4.0 is delegate. AI agents are becoming autonomous participants in commerce, and most businesses are invisible to them.

🔍

Invisible to Agents

Your website was built for eyeballs. Agents don't have eyeballs. They need structured data, APIs, and machine-readable service descriptions to find and understand you.

🔒

No Agent Authentication

When an AI agent wants to hire your service on behalf of a customer, can it authenticate and pay? Most businesses have zero infrastructure for agent-to-business transactions.

📡

No Discovery Protocol

Google indexed your HTML. Agents need service registries, capability manifests, and standardized schemas to discover what you offer and at what price.

⚡

Human-Only Workflows

Your checkout flow requires a browser, mouse clicks, and CAPTCHA solving. An agent with a wallet and a mandate can't complete your funnel. That's lost revenue.
